Who We Are

Founded in 1998 in the heart of Connaught Place, Spice Route has been serving Delhi's most discerning diners for over two decades. Our kitchen draws from recipes passed down through generations — dishes rooted in Mughal courts, Rajasthani havelis, and coastal fishing villages.

Every plate tells a story of a region, a tradition, and a season. We source our spices directly from the farms of Kerala, Rajasthan, and Himachal Pradesh, and our produce arrives fresh each morning from local mandis.
